# 🌍 TRACE — Trace your life

**Not just a map. A canvas for your coordinates, a timeline of your footprints, and the operating system for your memories.**

<br/>

> **We move through space, but we live in memories.** <br/>
> Most map apps treat locations as just points A and B—destinations to navigate toward and forget. TRACE treats locations as anchors for the human experience. It's a beautifully designed, relentlessly personal repository for where you've been, how it felt, and where you're going next.

<br/>

## ✦ The Philosophy

You don’t just "visit" a café; you have a breakthrough idea there. You don’t just go to a park; you watch a sunset that changes your perspective. 

TRACE is built on the belief that **space + time + emotion = memory**. By marrying the precision of geographic coordinates with the intimacy of a personal journal, TRACE becomes a living, breathing heat-map of your life. 

<br/>

## ✦ Features that feel like magic

### 🧠 Spatial AI Search (`⌘K`)
Forget scrolling through endless lists. Press `⌘K` and ask your map: *"Find me that quiet café with good wifi,"* or *"Where was that late-night biryani spot?"* TRACE instantly reads through your saved places, vibes, and notes to drop you exactly where you need to be.

### 📓 Geo-Journaling
Every coordinate has a story. Drop a pin and attach a memory. Over time, your map transforms from a sterile grid of streets into a rich, interactive diary. Scroll through your **Timeline** and relive your months not by dates, but by locations.

### ✈️ Trip Architecture
Stop planning trips in messy spreadsheets. TRACE lets you organize your itineraries geographically. Visualize your travel days, plot your spots, and check them off as you conquer new cities.

### 📊 The Analytics of You
Curious about your own rhythms? The **Stats Panel** provides beautifully rendered data on your life. 
- **Dynamic Heatmaps** showing your activity density.
- **Sparklines** tracking your exploration momentum.
- **Yearly Wrapped** insights detailing your most visited haunts, furthest adventures, and longest exploration streaks.

<br/>

## ✦ Designed to Disappear

We obsess over aesthetics so you don't have to. TRACE features a breathtakingly sleek, glassmorphic UI that floats weightlessly above your map. Whether you're in the deep `Dark` mode or the vibrant `Satellite` view, the interface gets out of your way and lets your memories take center stage. 

* Fast. 
* Fluid.
* Frictionless.

<br/>

## ✦ Make it Yours

Your data belongs to you. TRACE leverages deep local caching and seamless PWA (Progressive Web App) architecture to ensure your memories are available even when your cell service drops out on a remote mountain trail.
