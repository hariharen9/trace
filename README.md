## ✦ The Philosophy (The Why)

> **"We move through space, but we live in memories."**

Standard mapping applications are built for utility. They treat the world as a series of instructions: turn right in 200 feet, arrive at destination, erase the journey. They are built for machines, not for human beings.

**TRACE changes the equation.** 

TRACE is built on a simple premise: **Space + Time + Emotion = Memory**. It bridges the mathematical precision of geographic coordinates with the intimacy of a diary. By turning points on a grid into anchors for your stories, TRACE transforms a sterile map into a rich, living, three-dimensional tapestry of your life.

### 🧠 Spatial AI Search (`⌘K`)
Forget tags and checkboxes. Press `⌘K` and talk to your map like a friend. Our context-aware search engine scans your journals, vibes, and notes to drop you exactly where your memories reside.

### 📓 Geo-Journaling & Timeline
Every corner has a story. By logging entries directly onto coordinates, your travels turn into an interactive history. Use the **Timeline** to browse your past chronologically, or scroll the map to discover stories hidden in plain sight.

### ✈️ Trip Architecture
Break free from chaotic folders and cluttered spreadsheets. TRACE lets you organize your itineraries visually and geographically. Create distinct trips, assign distinct highlight colors, and trace your steps across cities and countries.

### 📊 The Analytics of You
Uncover the patterns of your curiosity. The **Stats Panel** provides elegant data visualization tracking:
* **Exploration Momentum**: See your exploration rate over time.
* **Vibe Frequency**: Understand the settings you gravitate toward (e.g., cafés, lookouts, trails).
* **Heatmap Densities**: Spot the areas where you've spent the most time.

## ✦ Designed to Disappear

We believe technology should amplify the real world, not distract from it. TRACE features a breathtakingly sleek, glassmorphic UI that floats weightlessly above your coordinates. 

* **Harmonious Palettes**: No plain primary colors. The interface uses modern HSL accents and deep charcoal base gradients.
* **Adaptive Map Views**: Toggle seamlessly between detailed Streets, high-altitude Satellite, topographic Outdoor, and dark minimalist styles.
* **Micro-Animations**: Hover states, active pin glows, and smooth transitions make the interface feel responsive, organic, and alive.

## ✦ Complete Data Autonomy

Your memories are yours alone. TRACE is designed to give you absolute control over your records:

* **Offline-First PWA**: Add TRACE as an app on your iOS, Android, or desktop home screen. Fully responsive layout overrides adapt automatically, letting you log spots off the grid or cache map tiles while exploring remote mountain trails.
* **Universal Share Target**: Share locations directly from Google Maps into TRACE. It parses coordinates, names, and links automatically, ready to save.
* **Seamless JSON Portability**:
  * **Export Backup**: Download your entire Firestore profile (places, journals, trips, collections) into a clean, lightweight JSON backup file with a single click.
  * **Import Merge**: Restore or transfer your backup file on any device. TRACE parses the file and merges items smoothly without creating duplicates.
* **Cloud Security**: Authenticate securely using Google login. All records sync in real-time to a secure Cloud Firestore instance tied exclusively to your account.
